MONIPUR COLLEGE

Monipur High School & College (Bengali: মনিপুর উচ্চ বিদ্যালয় ও কলেজ) is a secondary school and college located in Mirpur, Dhaka, Bangladesh. The school is also known as Monipur Uccha Viddalaya Ebong College (MUBC).
Principal: Md. Farhad Hossain
Founder: Hazi Noor Mohammod
Established: 1969
